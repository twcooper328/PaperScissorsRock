Read Me file for Paper-Scissors-Rock
By: Thomas Cooper - I.C.Stars C47

What is this:
This is a simple text/terminal based rock-paper-scissors game. 

How to play:
Enter your name - Enter your desired name. 
-Main Menu
Play - Move onto betting.
Tutorial - Quick refresher on how to play.

-Move quickly through the menus by entering "p"-

-Betting
more - add 10 coins to your bet.
less - reduce your bet by 10 coins.
all - go all in!

-Paper, Scissors, Rock
Enter either rock, paper, or scissor.
or simpy "r" "p" or "s"
play - Play against the computer.
